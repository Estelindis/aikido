Frequently Asked Questions


Yes! The vast majority of those who join our club have never learned a martial art before. We will help you to learn safely and comfortably. You will be shown each step of each technique clearly, as often as needed.


It's not necessary to wear a white suit ("keikogi") to train with us. If you do wish to purchase a suit, and you are a student, the club can order it for you and will usually pay part of the cost.


Most members of MU Aikido Club are students or alumni of Maynooth University. MU staff (and students, staff, and alumni of St. Patrick's College, Maynooth) can also join. If you don't meet any of these criteria, but you are over 18, you may train with us as a guest. We ask that guests join the Irish Aikido Federation for insurance purposes.

COVID-19


To help prevent the spread of COVID-19, training presently occurs in pods of six students (plus sensei). In classes with more than six students, members do not swap partners between pods when changing technique.


While restrictions continue, please wear a mask when walking the corridors of the sports hall.
